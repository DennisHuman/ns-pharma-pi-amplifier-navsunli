Dosage and Administration

Dose

For single-dose intracisternal or intraventricular infusion only.

The recommended dosage of NAVSUNLI is 2.9x1011 genome copies per gram of brain mass (GC/g) administered as a single-dose via intracistemal or intraventricular infusion.

The recommended dose in genome copies (GC), milliliters (mL), preparation volumes per route of administration, and the number of vials required as determined by brain mass range, is provided in Table 1.
