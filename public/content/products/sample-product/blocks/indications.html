Indication and Usage

NAVSUNLI is an adeno-associated virus (AAV) vector-based gene therapy indicated for the treatment of patients with mucopolysaccharidosis type II (MPS II; Hunter syndrome).

This indication is approved under accelerated approval based on 4-month reductions in CSF D2S6 biomarker (see Clinical Pharmacology). Continued approval for this indication may be contingent upon verification and description of clinical benefit in a confirmatory trial(s).
