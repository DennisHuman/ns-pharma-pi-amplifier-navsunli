Dosage and Administration

Patient Preparation

Prior to NAVSUNLI infusion:

  • Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) of the head/neck to determine the feasibility of intracistemal or intraventricular administration and calculate patient brain mass.
  • For intracistemal administration: perform lumbar puncture to determine opening pressure, withdraw cerebrospinal fluid equal to the volume to be infused, and inject radiocontrast agent.
  • Begin oral immunosuppressive treatment (see Table 2).

Table 2. Immunosuppressive Regimen Prior to and After NAVSUNLI Administration

Day as Related to NAVSUNLI administration Course of Immunosuppressive Treatment
Two days prior to NAVSUNLI administration Administer a loading dose of sirolimus 3 mg/m2/day divided in 3 doses (1 mg/m2 per dose) every 4 hours.
One day prior to NAVSUNLI administration Administer sirolimus 0.5 mg/m2/day divided in 2 doses with target blood level of 1–3 ng/mL.
On the day of NAVSUNLI administration Pre‑dose patient with methylprednisolone 10 mg/kg intravenously (maximum of 500 mg) over at least 30 minutes. The methylprednisolone should be administered before the lumbar puncture and intracisternal or intraventricular infusion of NAVSUNLI.

Administer oral sirolimus 0.5 mg/m2/day divided in 2 doses with target blood level of 1–3 ng/mL.
One day post‑treatment with NAVSUNLI Start tacrolimus oral administration:
  • Day 1 to Week 24: 0.05 mg/kg twice daily and adjust to achieve a blood level 2–4 ng/mL for 24 weeks.
  • Week 24 to Week 32: taper off tacrolimus over 8 weeks as follows:
    • At Week 24: decrease dose by approximately 50%.
    • At Week 28: further decrease dose by approximately 50%.
    • Discontinue at Week 32.
Continue dosing sirolimus: administer sirolimus 0.5 mg/m2/day divided in 2 doses with target blood level of 1–3 ng/mL.
Discontinue sirolimus at Week 48.
Two days post‑treatment with NAVSUNLI Start prednisone oral administration:
  • Day 2 to the end of Week 8: 0.5 mg/kg/day
  • Week 9 to Week 12: 0.35 mg/kg/day
  • Week 13 to Week 18: 0.2 mg/kg/day
  • Week 19 to Week 22: 0.1 mg/kg/day
  • Week 23 to Week 24: 0.1 mg/kg every other day
Discontinue oral prednisone after Week 24.
